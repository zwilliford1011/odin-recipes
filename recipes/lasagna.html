Lasagna

This is the recipe for perfect Lasagna!

  1. You will start by making the sauce!
    • Start making the ground beef and after it is browned add your favorite tomato sauce!
  2. Add sauce mixture to the bottom of the pan with a layer of Riccota cheese
  3. Add a layer of shredded mozzerella cheese!
  4. Add a layer of pasta noodles!
  5. Repeat until out of ingredients!
  6. Bake for 45 minutes and enjoy!
